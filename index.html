Class Objective

English kyun and kaise sikhni hai?

  • To communicate ‘man ki baatein’ to another person or a group of people
  • To be able to demonstrate knowledge or skills to a wider audience → videshi log and South Indians (because they do not understand or speak Hindi well)
  • To grow confidence → enables growth in career
  • To be able to teach kids their subjects at home
  • Eliminate fear of speaking English with strong foundation tools, games and other resources
  • Build wider and stronger vocabulary by self-questioning → “Isko English me kya kehte hain?”
  • Strengthen Grammar and Sentence Construction skills
  • Build effective communication and presentation skills with frameworks
  • Speak confidently on a topic or in any situation by mastering proven techniques
